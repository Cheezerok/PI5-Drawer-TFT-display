      TFT LCD       ||      Raspberry Pi
--------------------------------------------
	VCC         ->         3.3V
	GND         ->         GND
	CS          ->      GPIO 8 (CE0)
	RST         ->      GPIO 25
	DC          ->      GPIO 24
	MOSI        ->      GPIO 10 (MOSI)
	SCK         ->      GPIO 11 (SCLK)
	LED         ->        3.3V
